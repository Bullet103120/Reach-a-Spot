Fishing With Ma Bros

by: John Doe

      It has been our dream to travel to different places all over the world, excluding Philippines. So, we've searched and searched all around the web and the Philippines wouldn't want to be lost in track. As we scrolled on web pages this is one of the image that strucked our mind. Since then, it convinces us to "Let's give it a try" longing to see it. And this Reach-A-Spot in our hearts. We've come along with different thoughts giving us various negativity but it never looses our desire to see this tourists spot.

       As we travel on our way to the Philippines, Boracay is one of the recommended vacation spot that the market pointing us to visit. Our Preferrences, set us apart above the rest. We don't want a famous and crowded place. We value tranquility. We consider and value "Cultural Perspective". Adventurers and travellers has to consider all of this. And we are also frightened about the urban legend of "ASWANG HIDEOUT" as the elders describes the place.

       From the moment our plane landed we are so excited. However, You need to travel by the boat to Reach-A-Spot. We are astounded by the mountains and beaches that Panay has. As the saying goes it is a place where "The Mountains meet the Sea." Staring on these spectacular views is not enough once you stepped out of the boat you will hear local shoutouts saying "Kkkruhayyyy!!!" which means "Welcome". We first witnessed How the Lambaklad fishermen "FISHING" and yours truly joined them. Fishing with my Brothers was one of my memorable journey in Antique that Reach-A-Spot in my heart. That urban legend that it is an island of "ASWANG" has now wiped out. It was an amazing experience.
